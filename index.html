History of the Venice Project Center

Founded in 1988 by Fabio Carerra, the Venice Project Center gave WPI students an opportunity to apply their technical and non-technical skills to help the city of Venice.

The Pioneer Team Project

Fabio Carerra, a recent graduate of WPI, has a vision for his home city of Venice. He gets approval from WPI and creates the Bootstrap Project called "Feasibility Study of the Venice Project Center"
